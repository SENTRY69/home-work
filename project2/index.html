Sailing with the Scotts

Sailing with the Scotts - your chance to go cruising with Drew and Jonathan Scott—is back, and it’s bigger and better than ever! This December, the brothers invite you to sail with them from Miami to the Bahamas. Over five days and four nights aboard Royal Caribbean's newly renovated Mariner of the Seas®, enjoy design workshops, meet-and-greets, a behind-the-scenes look at the latest season ofBrother vs. Brother, themed parties, incredible food, and live entertainment, plus exotic ports of call in CocoCay and Nassau. Book now for boatloads of sun, fun, and surprises from your favorite brothers!
